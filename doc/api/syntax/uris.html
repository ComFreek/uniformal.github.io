MMT URIs

The general structure of MMT URIs is described here. api.Path is the base class of MMT URIs. The classes corresponding to MMT URIs are

  • LocalName,
  • Document Paths (DPath),
  • Module Paths (MPath) of the form DPath ? LocalName,
  • Symbol Paths (GlobalName) of the form MPath ? LocalName and
  • ContentPath, which is a common superclass of MPath and GlobalName, and
  • Component Paths (CPath) of the form ContentPath $ DeclarationComponent.

MMT is developed and maintained mostly by Florian Rabe.
